Óstante Ristorante Italiano

Located in Rome, Óstante offers all of the comforts of a cold, depressing wine cellar that one can possibly imagine while serving adequate italian food. We make all of our pasta in house, including:

  • Spaghetti
  • Linguine
  • Farfalle
  • Tetrazzini
  • Rigatoni
  • Cannelloni
  • Tortellini
